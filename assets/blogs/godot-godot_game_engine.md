# My godot journey

### why godot ?
#### TL,DR
> MIT licence
> export to windows/mac/linux/ios/android/html5...
> Vulkan rendering
> VR support
> easy and elegant
### What does it have the other don't ?
- an executable that is less than 100mb
- no string attached ( take it , rename it, resell it... nobody care :) )
- usability
### What the other have it don't ?
- nanite dynamic polygone rendering
- destroyable physics building
- easy export to xbox, playstation and nintendo
### is there a big enougth comunity ?
> YES

easy to learn! you can choose from books, videos and articles
big comunity! get help on Discord, Forums, IRC...
### is it production ready ?
> YES

to me it's ready since v3, yet v4 is where it enter the serious game.
spetially after after big donations from facebook and epic.
